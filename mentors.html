Mentors

If you're looking for advice or support about anything C++ related, our mentors will be happy to impart their knowledge and experience! Simply find a mentor with the relevant experience and get in touch with them, and they'll get back to you when they can.

If you're not sure who to get in touch with, contact the C++ Edinburgh team via email or Twitter, and we'll give you a hand in finding someone with the right experience to help you.

Want to join this list? Help out the local C++ community by joining us as a mentor, so that people can get in touch with you for help and advice.

Of course, we expect both parties to behave responsibly and in a friendly and inclusive manner according to our Code of Conduct.
